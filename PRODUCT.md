# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, engineering managers, and founders evaluating a Forward Deployed AI Engineer for high-stakes production AI work. They scan quickly, looking for evidence of shipped systems, scale, and technical depth. The portfolio must communicate competence and personality in one scroll.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for Faiq Hilman, an AI engineer who ships production GenAI systems to enterprise customers. The site exists to prove, not claim: every section shows what was built, at what scale, and with what stack. Success is a recruiter or founder reaching out after a 60-second scroll.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, warm. The engineer behind this site ships production systems for enterprises, but the site itself should feel personal, not corporate. Gold-on-obsidian luxury-tech with a Three.js orbital background. The voice is direct, no buzzwords, every word earns its place.

## Anti-references

- Generic boring SaaS with no personality (purple/blue gradients, glassmorphism cards, stock illustrations)
- Terminal aesthetic (green-on-black, mono everywhere, code-as-decoration)
- Soulless corporate themes (stiff grids, stock photos, blue and grey, no voice)

## Design Principles

1. Show, don't tell. Every claim is backed by a number, a stack, or a shipped artifact.
2. Expert confidence. The design knows what it is and doesn't hedge. No timid palettes or template layouts.
3. Grounded luxury. Gold and obsidian, not because it's trendy, but because it signals craft and precision.
4. One scroll, one story. The page reads top-to-bottom as a single narrative, not a menu of sections.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific requirements. Standard best practices: sufficient contrast, keyboard navigation, reduced motion respect where feasible.
